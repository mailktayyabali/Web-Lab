About Finders Keepers

Reuniting people with their lost belongings through the power of community

Our Mission

At Finders Keepers, we believe that every lost item has a story and deserves a chance to find its way home. Our platform connects honest finders with worried losers, creating a trusted community where lost items can be quickly reunited with their rightful owners.

We're committed to making the process of reporting and finding lost items as simple and efficient as possible, while fostering a culture of honesty and community support.

Our Story

Finders Keepers was born from a simple idea: what if there was an easy way to connect people who've lost something with those who've found it? After losing a cherished family heirloom and struggling to find it through traditional methods, our founder realized there had to be a better way.

Since launching in 2023, we've helped reunite thousands of people with their lost belongings, from everyday items like keys and phones to irreplaceable sentimental treasures. Each successful reunion reminds us why we do what we do.
